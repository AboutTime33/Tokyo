Day 1: Modern Tokyo

Morning visit to Odaiba: Palette Town, teamLab Borderless, and DiverCity Tokyo Plaza.
Lunch at a local tonkatsu (breaded pork cutlet) restaurant.
Afternoon at Shibuya: Shibuya Crossing, Hachiko Statue, and shopping in Shibuya 109.
Evening in Ebisu/Yakitori Alley: Try yakitori (grilled skewers) and explore cozy bars.



Remember, the key to exploring Tokyo like a local is to immerse yourself in the local culture, try authentic cuisine, and venture beyond the usual tourist spots. Be open to spontaneous interactions and follow the lead of locals to truly experience the vibrant energy of this incredible city.
